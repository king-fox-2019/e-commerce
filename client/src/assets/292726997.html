      A SHOE WITH YOUR KIND OF SOLE.

      The Nike Free RN Flyknit 3.0 By You lets you choose between 2 innovative sole options. If you like a more minimal feel, keep the 3.0. If you're looking for a little more cushioning, swap it for the 5.0. Finish your creation with a lightweight, breathable Flyknit upper in custom colours, with reflective elements or metallic accents and a personal message.

      SWAP YOUR SOLE

      Like the minimal feel of the 3.0? Keep the classic sole and enjoy a barefoot-inspired ride. If you want a little more cushioning to go with the sleek, slip-on design of the 3.0, try it with the comfy foam sole of a 5.0.

      SHINE IT UP

      Take your Swoosh to the next level with reflective elements or metallic finishes.

      MAKE IT PERSONAL

      Customise your shoe with your name, inspirational details or a special message lasered into the foam on the inside of your foot.

      Stretch and Security

      A Flyknit upper comes together with other synthetic materials for a second skin-like fit, while a slip-on design reduces pressure on your instep for total comfort.

      Structural Support

      An anatomical outsole follows the shape of your foot to provide supportive cushioning that doesn't get in your way, while heel-to-toe flex grooves bend with your foot. A strap across the midfoot provides stretchy structure to keep feet contained.

      Totally Grounded

      Free outsoles are designed to give you that close-to-the ground feel with heel-to-toe grooves for multi-directional flexibility. Whether you pick the 3.0 or the 5.0, single-density foam and a special sockliner will help you stay connected to that barefoot feel.
